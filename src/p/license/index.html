  • Video games (mobile, console, PC, web)

2.2 Modifications

You have full rights to modify, adapt, combine, and create derivative works from purchased assets for use in your projects.

Example:

You purchase a 3D character model. You can retexture it, modify the geometry, combine it with other assets, and use it in your commercial game. You can even create multiple variants for different characters in your game.

2.3 Team Usage

The license extends to your team members working on projects that use the purchased assets. Team members do not need individual licenses.

2.4 Client Work

You may use purchased assets in projects you create for clients, provided the assets are integrated into the final product and not distributed as standalone files.

4. Multiple Projects

A single purchase allows you to use the asset in unlimited projects. There are no per-project fees, no royalties, and no revenue sharing requirements.

Example:

You purchase a UI kit. You can use it in Game A, then reuse it in Game B next year, and again in Game C after that. No additional licenses needed.
