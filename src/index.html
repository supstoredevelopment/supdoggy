Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know about SupStore assets and licensing

Can I use these assets in multiple projects?

Absolutely! Once you purchase an asset, you can use it in as many projects as you want, including commercial games, apps, and experiences. There are no per-project fees or additional licensing required.

Are the assets optimized for mobile devices?

Yes! All our assets are performance-tested and optimized to run smoothly on mobile devices. We include multiple LOD (Level of Detail) versions and provide optimization guidelines to help you achieve 60 FPS on most modern mobile hardware.

How do updates work?

When you purchase an asset, you get lifetime access to all future updates and improvements for that asset at no additional cost. We regularly update our assets based on user feedback and studio updates.

Do you offer refunds?

No, we do not offer refunds.
